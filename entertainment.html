Entertainment

Each year the Gilroy Garlic Festival offers a wide variety of top quality entertainment, all included in the price of your Festival admission. With three stages and several groups of strolling musicians, you are never far away from the great sounds of jazz, rock 'n roll, country, blues, reggae, swing and much more. Don't miss the chance to dance and sing with several thousand people as happy and garlic-crazy as you!
